1. what sports did alan like
 According to the memoir, Alan Plush mentions enjoying the following sports:

1. Handball
2. Paddleball
3. Bowling
4. Ping-pong
5. Baseball (although he hated playing Little League)
6. Ice hockey (he was forced to play, but never enjoyed it)
7. Basketball (he was very good at and enjoyed playing)

Note that Alan also mentions his father being an avid athlete and participating in various sports, but the focus of the chapter is on Alan's experiences with these sports.
